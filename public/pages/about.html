About RazaTech

We craft tech that matters

A forward-thinking software company from Uganda, building digital solutions that empower businesses across East Africa and beyond.

Our Story

Built from passion, driven by purpose

RazaTech was founded with a singular mission: to bring world-class technology to businesses that need it most. Operating out of Kampala, Uganda, we've grown from a one-person operation into a full-service digital agency serving clients across East Africa and internationally.

We specialize in building robust web applications, SACCO systems, blockchain solutions, IoT platforms, and scalable database infrastructure. Every project we take on is treated as a long-term partnership — not just a transaction.

Our approach combines technical excellence with deep industry knowledge, ensuring we deliver solutions that don't just work, but genuinely transform how businesses operate.
